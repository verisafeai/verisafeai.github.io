Our Vision

Safe, controllable, and verifiable
autonomous AI agents

VeriSafe's core mission is enabling autonomous AI systems to operate safely in the real world — by design, not by accident. We achieve this through three foundational pillars.

🛡️

Safe

Formal guarantees prevent harmful actions before they happen. Our systems enforce safety constraints mathematically — no edge case can slip through.

🎮

Controllable

Human oversight remains active at every critical decision point. Operators retain authority and visibility into what autonomous agents are doing and why.

Σ

Verifiable

Mathematically verified policies backed by formal proofs. Our certified safe policies meet the strictest regulatory requirements by construction.

The Problem

Autonomous AI systems remain undeployed in safety-critical domains

The potential of autonomous AI agents is enormous — but deployment in high-stakes environments is blocked by a fundamental lack of trust, formal safety guarantees, and regulatory clarity.

⚠️

No Trust, No Deployment

Organizations cannot risk deploying AI agents in critical systems without provable safety assurances. Statistical accuracy is not enough — a single failure can be catastrophic.

📋

Regulatory Barriers

Emerging regulations (EU AI Act, ISO standards) require formal evidence of safety. Current AI systems cannot provide this, leaving compliance a blocker for entire industries.

🔮

Opaque Decision-Making

Black-box AI models make decisions that engineers cannot audit or predict. In safety-critical deployments, this unpredictability is unacceptable.
